## What shipped this cycle

### 1. `scripts/conv-loop.mjs` rewired to the generated MD corpus
- Imports `CORPUS` from `eval/generated/corpus.ts` (was: `CONVERSATION_CORPUS` from the now-deleted `eval/conversation-corpus.ts`).
- Compiles `{pattern, flags}` regex objects on the fly; reads `min_len`/`max_len` snake_case.
- Iterates `turn.role === 'user'` and skips/injects scripted assistant turns.
- Filters by `spec.id` or `spec.title`; `--pending` flag opts in pending cases.
- New `--seed N` flag instantiates the engine with `{ testMode: true, rng: mulberry32(N), now: () => 1700000000000 }`.

### 2. The 12 Thorsen TS personas ported to MD
Files added under `eval/corpus-md/{multi-turn,cognitive}/thr-*.md`:
`thr-nickname-prelude-mira`, `thr-tiny-followup-anders`, `thr-relative-dates-sigrid`, `thr-message-count-leon`, `thr-typos-helena`, `thr-teach-poison-johan`, `thr-buried-math-isak`, `thr-literal-meta-tone`, `thr-weather-refusal-eivind`, `thr-compound-multi-intent-ragna`, `thr-one-word-followup-bjorn`, `thr-greeting-plus-question-ida`. All 12 lint-clean and pass.

### 3. Dual source killed
Deleted `eval/conversation-corpus.ts` and `packages/core/__tests__/conversation-corpus.test.ts`. The MD generator is now the only corpus source.

### 4. Engine determinism knobs
Added to `VaiEngineOptions`:
- `testMode?: boolean` — disables `tryWebSearch` and `runSearchWithBudget` (returns `null` without touching the network). Constructor flag takes precedence over `VAI_TEST_MODE` env var.
- `rng?: () => number` — replaces `Math.random` in 6 response-path sites (greetings, jokes, suggestions, sentence shuffles).
- `now?: () => number` — replaces 5 response-path `Date.now` / `new Date()` sites in `tryUtilityQuestion` and the future-year guard.

A doc block at the top of `vai-engine.ts` documents the contract: test mode disables web; RNG and clock have their own injection points.

### 5. Capability gap analysis
`artifacts/corpus-runs/capability-gap-analysis.md` — per-row classification of all 22 failing cases with capability bucket, build-cost estimate, and confidence column. Aggregation table at the top. **No build-order recommendation** — that decision stays with the user.
